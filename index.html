What Is the Pet Pleaser

Has you pet been uncomfortable while you are doing such tasks like grooming? Pet Pleaser helps you groom your pet by keeping it in your lap and not scrambling away from you! You spread your petâ€™s favorite gooey substance on the tray, attach it to your leg and start grooming!

How To Use


1. Spread peanut butter (or whatever your pet's favorite gooey substance is).

2. Put the strap around your leg.

3. Put your pet on your lap.

4. Start grooming!

5. Once you're done, simply give it a quick rinse in the sink.
