What Is The Pet Pleaser?

Has your pet ever scrambled away from you while you are grooming him/her? If so, you should consider getting the Pet Pleaser. The Pet Pleaser straps around the user's leg and gives the pet positive reenforcement during grooming, nail trimming, bonding, or other interactions. You can put your pet's favorite gooey treat, such as peanut butter, into the container. The shapes like the paw, the bone, and the circle, will increase distraction time. Depending on how much treat used, It will last 5 - 20 minutes.
‍

How To Use

1.Spread peanut butter (or whatever your dog's favorite gooey substance is).

2. Put the strap around your leg.

3. Put Your Dog On your Lap

4. Once you're done, simply give it a quick rinse in the sink.

Choosing the right strap size
‍

Most people like wearing the Pet Pleaser from knee to mid thigh area. Measure the circumference of your leg using a measuring tape. If your measurement is 22 inches or less, the regular sized strap should be a pretty good fit. If the measurement is 22 - 32 inches, consider an extra long strap.
